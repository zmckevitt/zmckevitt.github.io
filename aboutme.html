Intro

I am currently an undergraduate Computer Science student at the University of Colorado Boulder. I expect to graduate with a BS in Computer Science and a minor in philosophy in Spring 2022. Presently, I am working as a research assistant in the Electrical, Computer, and Energy Engineering (ECEE) department at CU, where my work consists of using probabilistic programming models (using the WebPPL language) to determine timing side-channels in software. Upon completing my undergrad, I would like to pursue a PhD in CS or Math.
